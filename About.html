Steel & Industrial Materials Company Limited

Fortunate Steel & Industrial Materials Company Ltd. is a prominent supplier of a comprehensive range of high grade steel structural products. Our impeccable array of products includes: Mild Steel Plates, Steel Pipes, H.Beams, U.Channels, Galvanized Pipes, Stainless Steel, Angle Iron, Gratings, B. R. C. Wire, Rods, Chain link wire, Mesh, Flat Bars etc. The products (materials) that we supply are soured from the most reliable vendors & Manufacturers, and the products are in compliance with the international standards. For this reason we are very conscious of the quality, safety and durability of our goods and services and its commercial integrity as well as customer satisfaction. Responsibility encapsulates one of the core values of Fortunate Steel & Industrial Material Company Ltd. we take our responsibilities very seriously and translate that responsibility into measurable goals and attainable targets.
